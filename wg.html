Welcome to the IRCv3 Working Group

We're a group of IRC client and server software authors working to enhance, maintain and standardize the IRC protocol. Stakeholders in IRC such as IRC networks and network operators are invited to participate.

Participation

The IRCv3 Working Group contains participants from many top IRC networks and development teams, and welcomes new participants.
